About

Reporting from the center of American power

Caden Olson is a Washington reporter covering Congress, the White House, the Supreme Court, and national politics. He is one of two staff reporters at Matt Taibbi's Racket News, one of Substack's top independent political publications, where he also authors The Swamp Log — a weekly dispatch from the nation's capital.

Before Racket News, Caden spent six months as a Congressional Reporter at The Daily Caller, reporting daily from Capitol Hill and producing a widely-shared series of on-camera confrontations with sitting members of Congress and administration officials — including a 2.6 million-view interview with Rep. Nancy Mace filmed on the day of the assassination of Charlie Kirk.

He is a graduate of Colorado State University with a B.A. in Journalism and Media Communications (GPA: 3.87) and completed the National Journalism Center program in Washington, D.C.
